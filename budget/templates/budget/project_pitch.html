Pitch!

We want to work with you. Use this form to pitch us a project.

Give your idea a name and tell us just a bit about what it is. The description field is intentionally limited because we want to have a conversation with you, not exchange white papers. Give us a brief run-down now and we'll reach out to get the whole picture and take down the details together.

What are we looking for?

There is no formula, but the best pitches are specific. Tell us what data you have or want to collect or what story you want to tell. We want pitches for stories, data visualizations, interactive features, data-driven investigations and smart newsroom tools.

What happens next?

Deadlines permitting, we'll reach out within a day to talk with you about your pitch. Not sure you're ready to pitch but still have an idea worth talking about? Contact us in Slack.
